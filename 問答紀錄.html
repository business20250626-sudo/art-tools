Leonardo Image Reference 圖生圖 2026-07-03

用途

Image Reference 是「餵一張參考圖給模型」，讓生成結果參考它的風格、構圖、角色或內容，而不只是靠文字描述。適合想延續某個畫風、或想固定某個角色 / 物件時使用。

步驟

  1. 進入 Image 生成頁，在 prompt 輸入框左側點圖片的圖示，把參考圖上傳進來。
  2. 這次我們實作一個阿努比斯,選好模型及參考圖片後,輸入要生成的圖片描述,再按 Generate。
    Same art style as the reference image. A majestic depiction of Anubis, the ancient Egyptian god of the dead. He has a smooth black jackal head with long upright ears and glowing amber-gold eyes. His physique is tall, lean, and powerfully athletic. He wears an ornate black-and-gold pharaonic headdress, a broad gold collar inlaid with turquoise, and intricately crafted ceremonial armor inspired by ancient Egypt. Around his waist hangs layered flowing linen robes and royal fabrics decorated with gold ornaments and hieroglyphic patterns. In his right hand he holds a magnificent golden ceremonial staff topped with the sacred symbol of Anubis. In his left hand he holds an ancient khopesh sword. Gold ornaments decorate his wrists, upper arms, and ankles. Pure black background. Full-body character portrait. Standing upright, facing forward in a calm and dignified pose. Do not add any extra objects, creatures, effects, environment, smoke, particles, lighting props, jewelry, wings, capes, cloth pieces, symbols, or background elements beyond those explicitly described.

    • 阿努比斯的外觀描述
    • 背景與人物姿態描述
    • 限制描述,避免他生成沒有提到的內容

Leonardo 內容融合 多圖參考 2026-07-03

用途

想同時參考兩張圖，而且各自只取其中一部分——例如「A 圖的角色 ＋ B 圖的服裝／場景」。作法是放多張參考圖，再用文字 prompt 指定各自要保留的重點。

步驟

  1. 上傳兩張要參考的圖片
  2. 這次做圖片融合,輸入描述後按generate
    Use the machine cabinet design, overall structure, proportions, materials, colors, decorative details, and lighting style from Image 1. Replace only the central feature of the machine with the main element from Image 2, redesigned to seamlessly match the artistic style and visual language of Image 1. Integrate matching light bulbs around the central element using the same shape, spacing, materials, colors, and illumination style as the light bulbs on the machine in Image 1, making it appear as an original part of the machine. Use the background atmosphere, environment, lighting mood, and overall ambiance from Image 2. Keep the machine design from Image 1 unchanged except for the central feature. Do not alter the cabinet shape, frame, proportions, decorative ornaments, materials, or color palette. Blend all elements naturally into a single cohesive design with consistent style, lighting, and perspective.
    • 描述可以寫個大概丟到 ChatGPT 之類的地方幫忙修改
    • 將個別需要的元素拆解描述
    • redesigned to seamlessly match the artistic style and visual language of Image 1 ⇒ 不是直接把圖二的風格貼到機台中間，而是把圖二的元素重新設計成與圖一一致的美術風格
    • 如果有些細節沒做到、或要再加東西，就拿目前最好的圖針對沒做好的地方再次修改，不要要求一次到位

Higgsfield Video Reference Image Reference 換配色 2026-07-03

用途

想保留一支影片的動作 / 構圖，只把它的配色換掉。作法是用 Video Reference 鎖住動態，再加一張 Image Reference 當顏色來源，讓輸出影片套上參考圖的配色。

步驟

  1. 在 Higgsfield.ai 點擊+符號,選擇要參考的影片及圖片。
  2. 輸入替換的描述
    影片二換成圖一的顏色

成果對照

注意事項

  • 本次模型使用Seedance 2模型才用中文描述,部分模型對中文有較好的理解,大部分仍是英文為主
  • video reference會有最低秒數限制
  • 像本次範例的影片只有3秒,所以當第四秒時會發現外匡顏色有變化,可以根據用途做刪減或者參考影片長一點
Higgsfield Image to Video 圖生影片 2026-07-03

用途

手上已經有一張滿意的圖，想讓它動起來變成影片（鏡頭運動、人物/物件動作）。作法是把這張圖當作起始影格，再用 prompt 描述要怎麼動。

步驟

  1. 準備好要當起始圖的圖片（構圖、主體都確定的那張）。
  2. 輸入動作描述
    不斷地由右至左水平旋轉
